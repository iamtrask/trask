Researcher at Digital Reasoning, training the world's largest neural network (160B params). ICML.

BS in Applied Discrete Mathematics and BBA in Finance at Belmont University.

I'm the founder of OpenMined, working on technologies to increase the capability of and deconcentrate power over AI by combining deep learning, cryptography, and distributed systems. I'm particularly interested in attribution-based control for AI systems and broad listening. PhD student at Oxford. Research Scientist at Google DeepMind. Term Member at the CFR.
